FEATURES

Everything you need to wireframe

Not a kitchen sink. Just the tools that move a wireframe from idea to shippable in one surface.

  • Prompt to wireframe

    Type what you want and AI drafts the first screen. Powered by Gemini, with a local fallback for airgapped demos.

  • Component palette

    Click to insert or drag to drop. Buttons, forms, nav, cards — everything prebuilt, everything editable.

  • Realtime cursors

    See every teammate's pointer live. No refresh, no stepping on each other's edits.

  • Pinned comments

    Drop a comment anywhere on the canvas. Threads stay anchored to the pixel you pinned them to.

  • Workspaces

    Invite teammates with one link. Each tenant is its own isolated workspace with its own projects and members.

  • Activity feed

    Every change, edit, and comment in one tenant-wide stream. Catch up at a glance.
